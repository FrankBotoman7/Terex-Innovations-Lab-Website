Featured Project

Terex Part-Time Classes (PTC) Mobile App

The Terex Part-Time Classes (PTC) mobile application is an innovative e-learning platform designed to bridge the gap between skilled educators and eager learners. It functions as a video tutorial marketplace tailored for the African context, focusing on accessibility and affordability.

For Teachers: The platform allows subject matter experts and tutors to upload high-quality video tutorials on various topics, transforming their knowledge into a scalable income stream.

For Learners: Students can browse a diverse library of subjects and acquire lessons by paying a small, one-time fee per video. Crucially, the app allows learners to download the tutorials directly to their device, enabling crucial offline learning without needing continuous internet connectivity. This promotes flexible, affordable, and practical education for everyone.
